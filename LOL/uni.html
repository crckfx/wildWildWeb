Overview

This project was completed during a recent university trimester. We began by identifying a real-world problem we were experiencing and designed an app as a solution.
The assignment was split into three stages:

  • Stage One — Explored the problem space and identified possible solutions.
  • Stage Two — Developed the chosen solution into a working prototype.
  • Stage Three — Tested the prototype and iterated based on feedback.

The Five Y's

The Five Why's

Why would someone seek out products that they’ve had overseas?
traveler's often seek products they’ve experienced overseas due to nostalgia and sentimental value, unique flavour or superior quality, These products can bring back fond memories and offer taste not found locally. People who have previously lived in another country might seek out these products due to language barriers in english supermarkets, Familiarity, or to maintain their culture
Why would someone have to seek out these products?
These products might not be readily available or easily found in local stores. The unique or specialised nature of these overseas products often mean they are only sold in specific shops, online marketplaces, or ethnic grocery stores.
Why can’t you get these products at common supermarkets?
These products are often not available at common supermarkets due to their regional specificity. Supermarkets typically stock items with broad appeal. Unique products arn’t as commonly bought by the general population. Some products may require special storage conditions or sourcing arrangements that make it expensive to ship.
Why wouldn’t someone just order it online?
High shipping costs, long delivery times, and availability issues can make it inconvenient. Concerns about product quality and authenticity, import regulations, and customs duties can also interupt the delivery process . Additionally, ordering food and drinks online require extra organisation of being home to collect the delivery and knowing when you will need the products in advance.
Why wouldn’t you use at alternative that is similar?
The original product may have unique qualities that alternatives cannot replicate. These unique qualities can include specific flavors, ingredients, or preparation methods that are intrinsic to the original product. Emotional connections and sentimental value associated with the product also make in irreplaceable.
