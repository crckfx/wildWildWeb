Extended Content Mode (In Development)

The Extended Content toggle lets you control how much detail you see. ’Extended mode' reveals extra process insights with a smooth typing animation. Toggle it anytime, no reloads, no interruptions.

In my research, I found that a common issue with portfolios is either being overloaded with information or too minimal, lacking enough detail. To solve this, Im introducing a toggle that lets users choose the level of content they want to see. This keeps the experience immersive while also being respectful of the user's time.

Survey

The embedded survey system creates a continuous feedback loop, turning every interaction into real-time insight on what works and what’s unclear, and using that input to guide future iterations of the portfolio.

The feedback is submitted through a form connected to my Firebase backend. Each response is stored along with the:

  • Timestamp
  • Question answered
  • User's selected response

This setup allows me to review input in real time and understand how users are interacting with the portfolio. The data helps me identify what’s working and where improvements are needed, directly supporting the iterative goal of the project.
